Here at Supersonic Express we are dedicated to giving our passengers the rememberable experience they deserve. We work earnestly to ensure all passengers receive safety, luxury, and compassion. We have created a way for our passengers to learn about what we provide here before committing to us. Here at Supersonic Express we are more than just another airline. We are an experience.


Dining Reservations and Accomodations

Enjoy our array of various dining and snacking options included in each booking option through our pre-booking surveys! Each survey includes questions which pertain to microwave accessibility, feeding privacy rooms for new mothers, food allergy concerns, and the availability to choose from our breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacking menus.   We offer gluten-free, vegan, and vegetarian options, as well as the ability for passengers to program their own dietary needs into their surveys. Passengers with four or more members will be given a discounted “meal deal” within their purchase!

Available Booking Options

Choose your booking option from our five luxury cabin classes. We offer Economy, Premium Economy, Business, First, and Suite. Each option will provide you with a luxurious experience sure to be remembered. Economy and Premium Economy offer close seating with free of charge amenities, as well as comfortable legroom and reclining chairs. Business and First offers passengers with wider chairs, which they may to choose between private or public seating, along with free of charge amenities and care packages. Suite provides passengers with a private cabin room that includes a bed, shower, free of charge amenities, and care packages. For members who own a Cosmic or Intergalactic Membership Card, you will receive a reduced price on each flight you take and also be able to earn credit to put toward your next flight.

Activites and Services

Take advantage of the luxurious services and activities offered on your flight, including massages, guided meditation sessions, manicure and pedicure sessions, thousands of movies and shows to view, free WiFi and messaging available, a library of hardback and online books, and video games. Most activities can be chosen through our in-flight store. Select services and activities must be scheduled as appointments in the appropriate pre-booking survey, each of which will offer a time you are allowed to schedule each service and activity according to your flight schedule. Select services and activities may be subject to upcharge and are not offered in every booking option.


Health and Safety Practices

The health and safety of our passengers is our primary concern at Supersonic Express, which is why we commit to the practices of in-flight security and cybersecurity. We have trained doctors present on every flight, should an incident over occur, and we offer masks for those who are concerned about sickness. Our fleet of planes are routinely checked before and after each flight to ensure they are up to our highest standard and our pilots are seasoned and highly-trained to make the safest international flights. Each plane carries yellow wing hooks, oxygen masks, and fire-resistant chairs, so every passenger has access to safety in the case of emergencies. Our food and its preparation practices are always FDA and CDC approved for consumption. We protect the data information of our passengers, which is why we offer readily available WiFi in flight, use encryption code security, and never make contact with our passengers through other companies or services not associated with Supersonic Express.
